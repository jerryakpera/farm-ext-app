How FarmLink works

1

Create your account

Choose your role — Farmer or Extension Agent — and complete your profile in a guided three-step flow.

2

Register your farm

Add your farm's details — location, size, crops, and photos — to build your verified farm profile.

3

Post your questions

Ask anything about your crops, pests, or practices. Attach images to give agents the full picture.

4

Get expert answers

Certified extension agents respond, visit your farm, and share advisory content to keep your harvest thriving.
